## Overview

Performed 6 hands-on chemistry labs covering purification, analysis, and thermodynamic measurements.

## Experiments Conducted

### 1. Recrystallization Purification
- Purified unknown organic compounds using recrystallization technique
- Achieved **32.9% recovery**
- Identified substance as Acetanilide through mixed melting point analysis

### 2. Gravimetric Analysis
- Determined empirical formula of magnesium oxide
- Achieved 15.9% error margin

### 3. Dumas Method
- Calculated molecular weight of volatile liquids using ideal gas law
- Result: **74.6 g/mol**

### 4. Calorimetry
- Measured specific heat capacity of copper (**0.33 J/g°C**)
- Measured specific heat capacity of glass (**0.66 J/g°C**)

### 5. Hess's Law Application
- Determined enthalpy of reaction for Mg + H₂O system
- Result: **-305.3 kJ/mol**

## Technical Skills

Gravimetric Analysis, Calorimetry, Recrystallization, Thermodynamics, Laboratory Techniques
