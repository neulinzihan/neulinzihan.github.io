## Overview

Designed a scissor forklift powered by a power screw mechanism to help decoration workers reach high walls for painting and storage tasks.

## Problem Statement

Decoration workers often find walls too high to reach when painting. This project addresses the need for an affordable, stable indoor lifting solution.

## Design Features

- Force applied by **power screw mechanism**
- Strong stability for carrying a person or heavy objects
- Maximum rise height: **4 feet**
- Simpler structure than commercial products for household use
- Lower cost than market alternatives

## Applications

- Indoor wall painting assistance
- Storing items on tall shelves
- General household lifting tasks

## Technical Skills

SolidWorks, Mechanical Design, Power Screw Analysis, CAD Modeling
