Interviewing Hints / 面试技巧

中英文对照翻译 · Bilingual Translation

Introduction 引言
You always need to "take temperatures" because people have minds and they're changing them constantly. You need to listen to what they don't say. Being prepared for an interview is vital; the following preparation is very unique and effective in conducting a positive interview.
你需要时刻"试探温度"，因为人的想法是不断变化的。你需要听出他们没有说出口的话。面试前的准备至关重要；以下准备方法非常独特且有效，能帮助你进行一场积极的面试。
Things to Remember 要记住的事
People have to buy you before they buy from you.
别人得先认可你这个人，才会认可你的能力。
People hire and accept emotionally first and justify logically later.
人们先凭感觉决定录用，再用逻辑来合理化这个决定。
People are most sold by your conviction rather than by your persuasion.
打动别人的是你的坚定信念，而不是你的口才。
Know your technology, but think PEOPLE.
了解你的技术，但要以人为本。
The decision to hire is made in the first 5 to 10 minutes of the interview, with the remaining time spent justifying that decision.
录用决定在面试的前5到10分钟内就已做出，剩下的时间只是在验证这个决定。
The Candidate Preparation 候选人准备
Please take these notes to the interview and practice the anticipated questions that may be asked and your answers to those questions. Be sure to practice these steps out loud to yourself before the interview.
请把这些笔记带到面试中，并练习可能被问到的问题和你的回答。面试前一定要出声练习。
What are the duties and responsibilities of the position I'm applying for? This is an excellent icebreaker question for the hiring authority and a great start to a successful interview. What % of my job is dedicated to administration, supervisory, and technical? (should = 100%)
我申请的职位有哪些职责？这是向面试官提问的绝佳破冰问题，也是成功面试的良好开端。我的工作中行政、管理和技术各占多少百分比？（加起来应该等于100%）
What is my number one priority that has to be done before I leave each day? Why? (priorities are personal.)
我每天下班前必须完成的第一优先事项是什么？为什么？（优先级因人而异。）
What are the production or sales goals? What obstacles would prevent me from reaching my goals?
生产或销售目标是什么？有哪些障碍会阻碍我达成目标？
What are the short and long term goals set for the person in this position?
这个职位的短期和长期目标是什么？
Have questions for the hiring authority. Questions must be written out before the interview, while avoiding the topic of compensation and benefits for the first interview.
要准备好向面试官提问的问题。问题必须在面试前写好，第一次面试时避免涉及薪酬和福利话题。
Salary — this is a trap question. If the question is brought up a very good response is "I would like as much as the position will pay" OR "I am currently making $___. Although I would like an increase, I don't know enough about the opportunity to answer that fairly." Be very careful that you don't short yourself. Be sure to keep in mind your base salary, bonus program, stock options, gain sharing programs, performance bonuses, benefits, etc.
薪资——这是一个陷阱问题。如果被问到，很好的回答是"我希望获得这个职位所能提供的薪资"或者"我目前薪资是___美元。虽然我希望有所提升，但我对这个机会了解还不够，无法公平回答。"千万不要低估自己。要考虑到底薪、奖金计划、股票期权、利润分享计划、绩效奖金、福利等。
Ask for the job! "I haven't interviewed in a while, what is the next step? Can we conclude our business today if all goes well?" OR summarize what you've done that ties in with the new position and ask, "Do I have the qualifications you're looking for?" Then remain silent for an answer. If the hiring authority says, "I'm looking at other people," you say, "How do my qualifications match the people you're considering."
主动争取这份工作！"我有一段时间没面试了，下一步是什么？如果一切顺利，我们今天能达成吗？"或者总结你的经历与新职位的关联，然后问"我具备你们要找的资质吗？"然后保持沉默等待回答。如果面试官说"我还在考虑其他人"，你就说"我的资质与你们在考虑的人相比如何？"
(Your #1 priority is to receive an offer, if this is a position that you desire, your #2 priority is to know the next step.) ALWAYS SEND A FOLLOW-UP LETTER.
（你的第一优先是拿到offer，如果这是你想要的职位；第二优先是了解下一步流程。）一定要发跟进感谢信。
After you leave the interview, it is very important that you call us immediately.
面试结束离开后，请务必立即给我们打电话。
Self-Assessment Questions — Answer 20 or 30 of These 自我评估问题 — 回答其中20到30个
We guarantee that you will come up with some new ideas about your job responsibilities and skills.
我们保证你会对自己的工作职责和技能产生一些新的认识。
What experience, skills, aptitudes, or traits do you have, or think you might have, that could be of some use to some employer?
你有哪些经验、技能、天赋或特质是可能对雇主有用的？
What skills have you developed, at least to some degree, that you have never used at work?
你有哪些已经掌握但从未在工作中使用过的技能？
Do others, at work or elsewhere, come to you for any particular kind of help? What kind?
在工作中或其他场合，别人会来找你寻求某种特定的帮助吗？什么类型的帮助？
Do you have military experience (include Coast Guard and merchant marine)? Branch, grade, Specialty? Active duty, reserves, national guard? Discharge? Duties? Accomplishments? Medals, citations, commendations? Promotions ahead of schedule?
你有军旅经历吗（包括海岸警卫队和商船队）？军种、军衔、专业？现役、预备役、国民警卫队？退伍方式？职责？成就？勋章、嘉奖、表彰？提前晋升？
Have you ever published an article, report, or anything, even as a volunteer, even in your company professional association newsletter?
你是否发表过文章、报告或任何东西，即使是作为志愿者写的，即使是在公司专业协会通讯中发表的？
Have you ever given a talk, speech, or presentation, or provided training to anyone at work or elsewhere? Give the specifics.
你是否做过演讲、报告或培训？在工作中还是其他场合？请给出具体细节。
Computer literacy and related skills: What platforms can you use (PC, Apple, Atari, etc.)? Which one are you most comfortable with? What operating systems are you familiar with (DOS; Windows 3.x, NT, or 95; os/2; Unix; Apple; other)? If you program, which languages do you know, and what is your level of ability or experience in: What programs, or kinds of programs, have you designed or helped design or debug? What Internet research tools are you familiar with? What programs are you familiar with (word processors; spread sheets; data bases; groupware or PIM's, such as Lotus Notes, Groupwise, Ecco; graphics, desk-top publishing, etc.); office suites (Suite; Microsoft Office; Word Perfect Office); LAN or WAN system software? (If you know the latest version, mention it, as in "Lotus Notes v. 4." If you're not familiar with the latest version, give only program's name.)
计算机知识及相关技能：您能使用哪些平台（个人电脑、苹果电脑、雅达利电脑等）？您最习惯使用哪种平台？您熟悉哪些操作系统（DOS；Windows 3.x、NT 或 95；OS/2；Unix；苹果系统；其他）？如果您会编程，您了解哪些语言，您的编程能力或经验水平如何：您设计过哪些程序，或者协助设计过哪些程序，或者调试过哪些程序？您熟悉哪些互联网研究工具？您熟悉哪些程序（文字处理软件；电子表格；数据库；协作软件或个人信息管理软件，如Lotus Notes、Groupwise、Ecco；图形处理、桌面出版等）；办公软件套装（套装软件；微软办公软件；Word Perfect Office）；局域网或广域网系统软件？（如果您了解最新版本，请注明，例如“Lotus Notes v. 4”。如果您不熟悉最新版本，请只给出程序名称。）
What foreign languages do you know at least somewhat, and what is your level of skill in each: native speaker, fluent, moderate, phrase-book, write easily for professional purposes?
你会哪些外语？每种语言的水平如何：母语水平、流利、中等、短语级别、能熟练撰写专业文件？
What planning or analytical tools are you familiar with (critical path? PERT; quality function deployment; etc.)?
你熟悉哪些规划或分析工具（关键路径法？PERT；质量功能展开等）？
What experience have you had as a manager of or participant in TQM? cQi? Business process reengineering (which version: general structure/function analysis or computer systems analysis)?
您在作为 TQM 管理者或参与者方面有何经验？cQi？业务流程再造（是哪一版本：一般结构/功能分析还是计算机系统分析）？
Do you have any special travel experience, domestic or foreign? If you studied, lived, or worked in a foreign country, how long were you there? Did you live in an American enclave?
你有什么特别的旅行经历，国内还是国外的？如果你在国外学习、生活或工作过，待了多久？你是住在美国人聚集区吗？
